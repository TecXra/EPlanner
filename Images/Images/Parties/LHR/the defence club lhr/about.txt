The Defence Club offers an inspiring setting for hosting a variety of 
Banquets and private functions in three rooms of outstanding elegance. 
The Green Room and the Red Room have a seating capacity for 30 & 45 each,
 while the Banquet Hall accommodates 80 guests. The Club also provides a high 
standard of cuisine freshly prepared on site to cater for all functions hosted 
in the Banquet Hall and the two Party Rooms.
