Located on Main Kashmir Highway opposite to Nust University, Kiani’s Venue One is 
ideal place to host wedding related event. If you are planning your wedding and looking 
for nice and economical place for wedding, Venue One should be in your list of top places. 
Venue One Offers Quality Food, Marquee is well Decorated and Staff is very professional.

The average Price for Wedding event at Venue One is Rs 800-1000 Per head which include Food and Catering Cost.
